# Game Brief

Working title: Emberfall Online

High concept:
An original solo-developed online RPG inspired by the pacing, social feel, and progression philosophy of classic MMORPGs. Players begin as ordinary adventurers in a frontier region and grow through exploration, combat, quests, and social play.

Player fantasy:
- start weak, become capable
- live in a world larger than yourself
- earn upgrades through time and risk
- see other players in the same persistent space

Target session length:
- 15-30 minute short sessions
- 1-2 hour longer progression sessions

What MMO feel means here:
- always-online presence
- other players visible in the world
- local chat and emotes
- public enemies/resources/objectives
- readable nameplates and world activity

Vertical-slice target for v0:
- one starter zone
- login and spawn
- replicated player movement
- 3 enemy types + 1 named miniboss
- 3 quest chain
- auto attack + 1 active ability
- loot + simple equipment
- XP + level 2 progression
- local chat + basic emotes
- death + respawn
- reconnect persistence

Out of scope for v0:
- full class roster
- crafting professions
- auction house
- guilds
- raids or dungeons
- housing
- complex economy
