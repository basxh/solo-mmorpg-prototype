# Starter Zone

Zone name: Ashen Hollow

Biome:
Temperate frontier valley with old ruins, pine woods, shallow riverbanks, and a small fortified hamlet.

Purpose:
Teach movement, combat, questing, death/respawn, map usage, loot, and multiplayer presence in one compact area.

Landmarks:
- Cinderwatch Hamlet (spawn hub)
- Old East Road
- Weeping Pine Grove
- Broken Watchtower
- Shallow Ford
- Emberglass Ruins

Enemy families:
- Hollow Wolves
- Mossbound Scavengers
- Ashen Wraiths
- miniboss: Thornmaw Alpha

NPC roles:
- Marshal / guard captain
- quartermaster / gear vendor
- field herbalist / lore giver
- scout / quest guide
- ferryman or bridge warden

Quest chain outline:
1. Arrival at Cinderwatch: talk to Marshal, learn map and road
2. Keep the Road Clear: defeat 6 Hollow Wolves
3. Lost Satchel: recover an item near the Shallow Ford
4. Echoes in the Ruins: defeat Ashen Wraiths
5. Hunt Thornmaw: kill the named miniboss

Loot themes:
- worn frontier weapons
- leather boots and cloaks
- simple charms/trinkets
- ruined relic fragments

Design rules:
- no copyrighted locations or names
- no more than 5 core quests in the first slice
- every landmark must support either a quest or a navigation lesson
